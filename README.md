# Fashion Supply Chain Transparency

A comprehensive blockchain solution for tracking fashion products through their entire supply chain, ensuring transparency in manufacturing, labor conditions, and environmental impact.

## Key Features

• **Product Lifecycle Tracking** - Complete traceability from raw materials to finished products
• **Labor Condition Verification** - Worker safety scores, fair wage compliance, and child labor monitoring
• **Environmental Impact Monitoring** - Carbon footprint, water usage, and sustainability metrics tracking
• **Consumer Transparency** - QR code integration for product verification and transparency reports
